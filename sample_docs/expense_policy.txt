# TechCorp Expense Reimbursement Policy

## 1. General Guidelines
All business expenses must be pre-approved by your manager before incurring. Expense reports must be submitted within 30 days of the expense date. Original receipts are required for all expenses above $25.

## 2. Travel Expenses

### Flights
- Economy class for flights under 6 hours
- Premium economy for flights over 6 hours
- Business class requires VP approval
- Book through the company travel portal (TravelDesk) for best rates

### Hotels
- Maximum of $200/night for domestic travel
- Maximum of $300/night for international travel
- Exceptions require director approval
- Book through TravelDesk or approved booking platforms

### Ground Transportation
- Uber/Lyft preferred over taxis
- Rental cars only when public transport is not feasible
- Mileage reimbursement: $0.67 per mile for personal vehicle use

## 3. Meals and Entertainment

### Daily Meal Allowance
- Breakfast: $15
- Lunch: $25
- Dinner: $45
- Alcohol is not reimbursable except for client entertainment

### Client Entertainment
- Pre-approval required for expenses over $100
- Maximum $75 per person for client meals
- Entertainment must have clear business purpose
- Names of all attendees must be documented

## 4. Office Supplies
- Supplies under $50 can be purchased directly
- Supplies over $50 require manager approval
- Use approved vendors from the procurement portal
- Keep all receipts for tax purposes

## 5. Expense Submission Process
1. Collect all receipts and documentation
2. Log into ExpenseTracker portal
3. Create a new expense report
4. Attach scanned receipts
5. Submit for manager approval
6. Reimbursement processed within 10 business days

## 6. Non-Reimbursable Expenses
- Personal entertainment
- Traffic violations and parking tickets
- Airline upgrades (unless pre-approved)
- Mini-bar charges
- Gym or spa services
- Personal phone calls
- Childcare expenses
